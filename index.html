Forex Arbitrage Engine

During Bank of America Merrill Lynch Hackathon, built an arbitrage engine that can detect profit from the difference in bid & ask from different liquidity providers & execute trades to maximize profits.

Arbitrage engine was a client that connected to the different liquidity providers (servers) with socket.io to enable real-time event based communication due to the frequency of updates for the liquidity providers.

I built up the connection between the Java Arbitrage Engine (client) to the NodeJS servers (liquidity providers) using socket.io.

Java, NodeJS, Web Socket

OnDmand

With a friend built OnDmand, OnDmand is a web app platform that allows employers and part-time job seekers to connect with each other.

Employers can post their part time job vacancies and part-time job seekers can login to chat with the employers in the chatrooms to negotiate. Part-timers can also choose to accept or reject the job on the forum posting.

I built OnDmand's backend features with NodeJS, MYSQL & chatroom with socket.io

NodeJS, MYSQL, socket.io
